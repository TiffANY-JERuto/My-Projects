Chicken Bhajia

Description

Heard of potato bhajia, onion bhajia and fish bhajia? Well I discovered a new recipe online! Very simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup gram flour
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp green chillies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp tumeric powder
  • 3/4 cups water
  • 1/2 cup Daawat Basmati Rice,cooked
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • Oil,for deep frying

Directions

  1. Blend together rice, gram flour, ginger paste, green chillies, tumeric powder, salt and water in a blender until it is all combined.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the chicken, blended paste and coriander leaves and mix well.
  3. Drop coated chicken into hot oil at a time and let fry for 5 to 6 minutes. Take the chicken out on a paper towel to drain excess oil and then serce with your favourite sauce.
